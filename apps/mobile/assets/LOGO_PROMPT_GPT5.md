# 🔥 Logo Prompt für GPT 5.1 - KREATIV & ENERGETISCH

## 📋 Prompt (Komplett kopieren & in GPT 5.1 einfügen):

```
Create a bold, energetic app icon for a smoking cessation app. Think modern, fluid, powerful - not technical or over-designed.

FORMAT: Rounded square, 1024x1024 pixels.

BACKGROUND: Deep, rich black (#000000) - pure darkness that makes the flame pop. Maybe the tiniest hint of teal glow at the edges, but keep it minimal.

THE FLAME - FLUID & POWERFUL:
One unified, flowing flame shape - NO split, NO gap, NO harsh edges. Think of liquid fire, molten energy, a smooth teardrop of pure energy. The flame should feel ALIVE - organic, dynamic, moving upward with purpose. Wide at the base, tapering to a sharp, energetic tip. Smooth curves, no jagged lines, no excessive contours. It should feel like it's breathing, pulsing with energy.

COLOR & LIGHT:
Vibrant teal (#2DD4BF) as the core - bright, electric, impossible to ignore. Use smooth gradients, not hard facets. The flame should glow from within - brightest at the core, slightly darker at edges. Maybe a subtle inner light that's almost white/cyan at the very center. Think luminous, not faceted. The teal should feel electric, energetic, alive.

STYLE - MODERN & MINIMAL:
Forget geometric facets and hard edges. This is about FLOW. Smooth gradients, soft edges, organic movement. The flame should feel like it's made of pure energy, not folded paper. Less is more - one beautiful shape, glowing with life. Think contemporary art, not technical illustration.

COMPOSITION:
Flame centered, bold, confident. Takes up 70% of the space. Pointing straight up - determined, unstoppable. The negative space should feel intentional, not empty. This flame commands attention.

MOOD:
Raw energy. Pure motivation. A streak that burns bright. This isn't gentle - it's powerful, determined, unstoppable. The flame represents your fire, your commitment, your burning desire to succeed. Make it feel ALIVE.

TECHNICAL:
1024x1024px, rounded square. Must work at tiny sizes. No text, no smoke, no embers. One unified flame. Smooth, fluid, glowing. Background is pure black (#000000). Flame is vibrant teal (#2DD4BF) with smooth gradients and inner glow.
```
