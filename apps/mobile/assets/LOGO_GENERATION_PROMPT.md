# 🎨 Logo Generation Prompt für KI-Bildgenerierung

## Haupt-Prompt (für DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, etc.):

```
Create a modern, minimalist app icon logo for a smoking cessation app called "Nicht Rauchen". The design should feature:

VISUAL CONCEPT:
- A stylized, elegant flame icon as the main symbol - representing the "streak" (consecutive smoke-free days), motivation, and keeping the fire alive (staying smoke-free)
- The flame should be simple, clean, and recognizable at small sizes
- Inspired by modern icon design (similar to Ionicons flame icon style)
- The flame can be slightly stylized with a subtle upward-pointing, energetic movement
- The flame represents: your streak burning bright, your motivation, your commitment to staying smoke-free
- Centered, balanced composition with plenty of breathing room

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary accent color: Teal/Turquoise (#2DD4BF, hex: 2DD4BF) - vibrant, fresh, energetic, representing your active streak
- Secondary teal shades: Darker teal (#0F766E) for depth and shadows at the base of the flame, lighter teal (#14B8A6) for the bright tip/highlight
- Background: Very dark, almost pure black (#0A0A0A or #000000) - much darker, minimal blue undertone
- Optional: Subtle teal glow around the flame for depth and energy
- Optional: Very subtle gradient on the flame itself (darker teal at base to brighter teal at tip)

STYLE:
- Modern, flat design with subtle gradients
- Minimalist, clean, professional
- App icon style (suitable for iOS App Store and Google Play)
- Slightly rounded square format (1024x1024 pixels)
- No text, symbol only
- High contrast for visibility at small sizes
- Premium, sophisticated aesthetic
- Similar to modern icon libraries (Ionicons, Material Icons style)
- The flame should look energetic and positive, not destructive

COMPOSITION:
- Centered composition
- The flame icon is the main focal point, taking up approximately 60-70% of the icon space
- Negative space is balanced and breathing room around the flame
- The flame should be clearly visible and recognizable even at 20x20 pixels
- The flame should point upward, symbolizing progress and growth

MOOD & FEELING:
- Energetic, motivated, determined
- Clean, modern, professional
- Positive and empowering
- Represents strength, commitment, and the burning desire to stay smoke-free
- Premium and trustworthy
- The flame should feel like a "good fire" - your streak, your motivation, your success

TECHNICAL:
- Square format, 1024x1024 pixels
- Suitable for app icon (will be scaled down to various sizes)
- Vector-style or high-resolution raster
- Clean edges, no blurriness
- Optimized for very dark background (#0A0A0A or darker)
- The flame should have enough contrast to stand out on the dark background
- The flame shape should be simple enough to be recognizable at tiny sizes

AVOID:
- No text or letters
- No cigarette imagery or smoking-related symbols
- No negative imagery (skulls, warning signs, etc.)
- No cluttered elements
- No overly complex details that won't scale down well
- No realistic fire with smoke or embers
- Keep it simple, iconic, and positive
- The flame should not look dangerous or destructive
```

## Farb-Codes für Referenz:

- **Primary Teal:** `#2DD4BF` (RGB: 45, 212, 191) - Hauptfarbe für das Flame-Icon (heller Bereich/Tip)
- **Dark Teal:** `#0F766E` (RGB: 15, 118, 110) - Für Schatten und Tiefe (Flame-Base)
- **Light Teal:** `#14B8A6` (RGB: 20, 184, 166) - Für Highlights (Flame-Tip)
- **Background (NEU - viel dunkler):** `#0A0A0A` (RGB: 10, 10, 10) oder `#000000` (RGB: 0, 0, 0) - Fast schwarz
- **Optional Background Alternative:** `#050505` (RGB: 5, 5, 5) - Sehr dunkel mit minimalem Grau

## Symbolik der Flamme:

- **Streak:** Die Flamme brennt für jeden rauchfreien Tag
- **Motivation:** Das "Feuer" der Entschlossenheit
- **Erfolg:** Je länger die Streak, desto heller brennt die Flamme
- **Positiv:** Eine "gute Flamme" - dein Erfolg, nicht Zerstörung

---

## Nachbearbeitung-Tipps:

1. **Kontrast prüfen:** Stelle sicher, dass das Logo auch bei sehr kleinen Größen (z.B. 20x20px) erkennbar ist
2. **Rundungen:** iOS/Android runden App-Icons automatisch ab - teste das Logo in einem runden Rahmen
3. **Verschiedene Größen testen:** 1024x1024, 512x512, 256x256, 128x128, 64x64
4. **Export:** Als PNG mit transparentem Hintergrund (falls gewünscht) oder mit dem dunklen Hintergrund
