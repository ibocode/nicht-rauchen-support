# 🔥 Expert-Level Logo Prompt für GPT/DALL-E

## 🎯 Haupt-Prompt (Kopieren & Einfügen in GPT/DALL-E):

```
Create a premium, modern app icon for a smoking cessation app called "Nicht Rauchen". The design should be a rounded square icon (1024x1024 pixels) with the following specifications:

VISUAL STYLE - FACETED 3D DESIGN:
- The central element is a stylized flame icon rendered in a faceted, geometric 3D style
- The flame should appear as if made of folded paper or geometric planes, with distinct facets/segments
- The flame has two main lobes: a larger, more rounded left lobe (main body) and a smaller, pointed right lobe (flicker/tip)
- Each lobe should be composed of 2-3 distinct triangular or quadrilateral facets
- The facets should have subtle shading to create depth and dimension (some areas slightly darker, some lighter)
- The overall shape should be clean, minimalist, and recognizable as a flame even at small sizes

COLOR PALETTE - TEAL GRADIENT:
- Background: Very dark, almost pure black gradient (#0A0A0A to #000000) - subtle vertical gradient from slightly lighter at top to pure black at bottom
- Flame color: Vibrant teal/turquoise gradient (#2DD4BF) - the main flame body
- Facet shading: Use darker teal (#0F766E) for shadowed facets and lighter teal (#5EEAD4) for highlighted facets
- The flame should transition from darker teal at the base to brighter teal at the tip
- Optional: Very subtle teal glow around the flame edges for depth

COMPOSITION:
- Rounded square format (iOS/Android app icon style)
- The faceted flame is centered, taking up approximately 60-70% of the icon space
- The flame should point upward, symbolizing progress and motivation
- Balanced negative space around the flame
- The two-lobed structure creates visual interest while remaining simple

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Format: 1024x1024 pixels, square
- Style: Modern, premium, app store quality
- Faceted/geometric 3D appearance (like origami or folded paper)
- Clean edges, no blurriness
- High contrast between flame and dark background
- Suitable for scaling down to 20x20 pixels while remaining recognizable

MOOD & AESTHETIC:
- Premium, sophisticated, modern
- Energetic and positive (represents streak and motivation)
- Professional yet approachable
- The faceted style adds a contemporary, tech-forward feel
- Should feel like a "good fire" - success, achievement, burning streak

AVOID:
- No realistic fire with smoke or embers
- No orange/red colors (use teal only)
- No text or letters
- No cigarette imagery
- No overly complex details
- Keep the faceted style clean and geometric, not chaotic
```
